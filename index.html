What is Tech Developer Profile and Experience Card?

Tech Developer Profile and Experience Card is an open-source project for gathering facts about developer's journey, adventure, software engineering and software development in general.

Collection is done by developers and for developers around the world to shear their experiences. Remember it is Open-source! I do know you have a fact to tell about your journet so far. Why not shear that fact or experience to the world?
